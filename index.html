Ewelina Gajewska

Text Processing Research | Data Science


Ewelina Gajewska has completed a master's degree in cognitive science at Adam Mickiewicz University. During her master's studies she was a research assistant in international and National Science Centre funded projects (Volkswagen Stiftung, SONATA) working on computational modelling of arguments in natural language. Since 2023 Ewelina is a PhD student in Laboratory of The New Ethos at Warsaw University of Technology as part of the international iTRUST (CHIST-ERA) project, focusing on machine learning, natural language processing and social media analytics in the context of argumentative conflict, hateful language and social polarisation.
