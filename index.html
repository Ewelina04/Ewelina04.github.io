Ewelina Gajewska is a master's student in a cognitive science program at Adam Mickiewicz University in Poznan, Poland. She is a student research assistant in a National Science Centre (NCN)-funded project working on computational pathos and international projects focusing on analysis of conflict and polarization in social media in the Laboratory of The New Ethos at Warsaw University of Technology. Her research interests center around computational social science combining natural language processing (NLP) and psychology research.
